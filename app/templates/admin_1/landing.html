Intelpol empowers police departments and

citizens around the world with intelligence

to predict crime in real time

Powered by artificial intelligence, Intelpol is a fusion

of image, object, voice recognition technologies

allowing Police forces accurate predictions on type,

place and time of future crime.

Intelpol is designed to combat evolving

criminal activities in the digital age.



PREDICTING CRIMES

Forecast places and times with an increased risk of crime.



PREDICTING OFFENDERS

Identify individuals at risk of offending in the future.



PREDICTING PERPETRATORS

Machine learning techniques used to create profiles that
accurately match likely offenders with specific past crimes.



PREDICTING VICTIMS

Identify groups or, in some cases, individuals who are likely to become victims of crime.
