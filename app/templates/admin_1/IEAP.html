Together Let's develop policing platform for 21st Century

Why become part of IEAP?

✔ You’ll play a pivotal role in the validation of Intelpol platform and technology components.

✔ You’ll have access to the newest solutions and the opportunity to provide suggestions on the features of new solutions/components before they’re released, giving you a say in how the final solution turns out.

✔ You’ll also benefit from a dedicated IEAP expert who’ll assist you during installation and updates, and throughout the program.

How does the early adopter program work?

IEAP provides law informant agencies make sure Intelpol works for them before committing to a long-term contract. For a nominal fee, an Intelpol product specialists will work with you to assess the business use case, requirements, and configure the platform to meet your objectives.

Within a few days you can begin validating results of predictive policing. If necessary, settings can be tweaked over the next few weeks until things are just right. Once you're comfortable with the results, you can begin using the system operationally. You then receive 30 days of free subscription service to prove that it works for you. After all, software doesn't directly reduce crime rate.
